Summary

*Inception* is a mind-bending sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The film follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in stealing secrets from within the subconscious during the dream state. Offered a chance to erase his criminal past, Cobb must perform the impossible task — inception: planting an idea instead of stealing one.

Actors

  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames
  • Ken Watanabe as Saito
